About Us

Project overview:


This project summary is to manufacture a smart helmet for workers on hazardous construction sites with several features, including tracking the worker's location, falling detection, measuring temperature and humidity in the air, object detection, and checking gases that can cause ignition in the atmosphere. The project must be implemented to maintain the safety of workers and reduce the incidence of accidents and injuries. Providing the advantage of monitoring workers remotely for supervisors or site mentors. The project does not require external parties or official communications.

Objectives:

  • To reduce the risk of injury while working on work sites.
  • To reduce effort and time.
  • Protect workers and avoid the risk of injury while performing their jobs.
  • Data Analytics and Reporting, Implement data analytics capabilities to track safety metrics.
  • Environment Monitoring, Include sensors to monitor air quality for general safety.
    		•

    Scope:


    The project falls within the realm of wearable technology and IoT devices in the construction industry. It combines elements of sensor technology, real-time data analysis, and communication systems to enhance worker safety and efficiency on construction sites.
